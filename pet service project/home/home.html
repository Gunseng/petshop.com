What is pure msm for pets used for?

MSM is an organic compound that comes in the form of a white crystalline powder (methylsulfonylmethane). MSM is widely acclaimed by veterinarians and horsemen for its contribution to health care and horse ailments. It is important to supplement most diets with pure MSM. It is an excellent source of natural sulphur, an element that is important in the formation of connective tissue for pets and horses.
